Procedure

1.      Take 5mL of aqueous sodium sulphate solution in a test tube A.

2.         Take 5mL of aqueous barium chloride solution in a conical flask B.

3.         Record the mass of A and B  together before mixing them.

4.         Mix A and B, chemical reaction will take place and white ppt of BaSO4 (barium sulphate) will form.

5.        Record total mass of A and B together after the reaction.
