Animated activity on determination of pH

ANIMATION

How to run animation?
1. Select the solution from the drop down menu.
2. Drag the green pH sensor up to the solution in the jar.
3. To see the effect of dilution click on the lower tap to reduce the solution or click the upper tap to add water and observe.
