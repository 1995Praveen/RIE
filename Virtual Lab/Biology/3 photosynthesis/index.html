Photosynthesis

Objectives

To study that light is essential for photosynthesis.

Theory

All autotrophic plants prepare their own food with the help of sunlight,CO2, water and chloroplast .Chloroplast can absorb light and convert it into chemical energy . During the process of photosynthesis important events are – absorption of solar energy and its conversion into chemical energy, splitting of water and release of oxygen and reduction of CO2 into carbohydrate. The most important step is absorption of light. Light is one of the essential requirements of this process.

Q 2 What happens if you do not destarch the leaf properly?

Answers

(a)You will not get blue colour with iodine

(b) Leaf will dry

(c) Leaf will show presence of starch in all parts .

(d) Leaf will fall down from the plant .

Question

Q 3 Why should we decolourise the leaf before testing for starch?

Answers

(a) The green colour of the leaf will interfere with the blue black colour of starch with iodine .

(b) The green colour of the leaf mask the blue black colour of starch with iodine .

(c)Both

(d)None of these.
