Respiration

Objectives

To show that carbon dioxide is released during respiration

Theory

Every plant & organism performs respiration to get energy from the food. Food is oxidized in the body of organism to release energy. During respiration glucose is broken down. It release energy and carbon dioxide Respiration is of 2 types Aerobic and anaerobic . Aerobic respiration takes place in presence of oxygen. During aerobic respiration energy is released in the form of ATP and carbon dioxide and water are released. Anaerobic respiration takes place in absence of oxygen . In this process alcohol is released along with energy. It occurs in yeast and in certain microbes.

Steps involved in the respiration are

(1) intake of oxygen

(2)Oxidation of glucose

(3) Release of energy

(4) Release of Carbondioxide and small amount of water

 

This experiment is showing the release of carbon dioxide during aerobic respiration .
