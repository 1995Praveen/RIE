Human cheek cells

Objectives


To study the structure of squamous epithelial cells of human cheek.

 

 

 

 

Theory

 

All the living organisms are made up of cells.

New cells are formed from pre existing cells.

Cell is structural & functional unit of life.

Plant cells are covered by cell wall and provide it a rigid shape.

Question

Q 1 What type of cells are present in human cheek ?

Answers

(a)Ciliated epithelial

(b)Squamous epithelium

(c)Stratified epithelial

(d)Columnar epithelial

Question

Q 2 What difference will you see out of the following to differentiate animal & plant cell ?

Answers

(a)Cell covering

(b)Nucleus

(c)Nucleolus

(d)Mitochondria

Question

Q 3 Arrange the following steps in correct sequence–

(i) Take out a scrap from inner side of cheek

(ii) Place a coverslip on the tissue,

(iii)Add a drop of glycerine , and

(iv)Add stain to the tissue

Answers

(a) i, ii, iii, iv

(b) ii, i, iv, iii

(c) i, iv, iii, ii

(d) ii, iii, i, iv

Question

Q 4 Why should rinse our mouth properly before taking out the cells from mouth?

Answers

(a)It may protect the cells.

(b)It will remove any food particle if present.

(c)It will make you comfortable .

(d)none of the above.

Question

Q 5 What stain do you use to stain the cells ?

Answers

(a) Safranin

(b)Acetocarmin

(c)Methylene Blue
