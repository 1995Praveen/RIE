ABOUT US

There is a growing need to make use of cost-effective virtual labs to offer sciences at a distance. Keeping this in view, with the visionary approach of the Coordinator, Mrs. Rashmi Singhai, Assistant Professor RIE, Bhopal, this package has been developed by expert faculties of the Regional Institute of Education, Bhopal, along with specific science teachers from Kendriya Vidyalayas and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas. The amalgamation of ICT experts and subject experts in three phases has resulted in comprehensive, encyclopedic virtual lab simulations for better understanding and developing practical skills.

This package is developed as a learning resource for academic purposes in the free domain. The viewers can visit the blog vlabriebpl.blogspot.in for feedback & queries.
