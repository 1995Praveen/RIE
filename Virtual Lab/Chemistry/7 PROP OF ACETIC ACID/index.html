To study physical and chemical properties of acetic acid (ethanoic acid).

OBJECTIVES :

1. To study about Physical and chemical properties of acetic acid.

2. To observe and understand test for acid by litmus test.

3. To understand the decomposition of salts either sodium carbonate or sodium bicarbonate by acetic acid and also test for carbon di oxide by turning lime water milky.

4. To develop observational skill .

5. To develop skill to write and balance chemical reaction.

THEORY-

Ethanoic acid (CH3COOH) is an organic acid containing (-COOH) functional group.
It has an odour of vinegar.
It turns blue litmus paper red 
It is soluble in water due to formation of hydrogen bond.
 It reacts with sodium hydrogen carbonate and sodium carbonate to evolve carbon dioxide gas.

CH3COOH (l) + NaHCO3 (s) ⎯→ CH3COONa (aq) + CO2 (g) + H2O (l)
2CH3COOH (l) + Na2CO3 (s) ⎯→ 2CH3COONa (aq) + CO2 (g) + H2O (l)
